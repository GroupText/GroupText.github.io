Welcome!

This webpage was creating for the TGIS 505 Cartography and Data Visualization class for the Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies at the University of Washington - Tacoma.

These collaborative quarter-long group projects utilized creative representations of cartographic data for four different topics:


'What is a map?' is visualized by the Campus Map. This map breaks down a map to it's most simple form to show relationships that aren't initially seen.

'Remotely Sensed' is visualized by the Remotely Sensed map. This takes an area in a 3D printed form and apply a 2D Land Type Classification, combining the two forms together.

'Visualizing Text' is visualized by Map a Novel. This map shows hidden narratives from the Puget Sound Indian Wars and brings it into view.

'Mapping the invisible' is visualized by Mapping a Day. This visualization creates a temporal map of emotions for two 24-hour periods.
